Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • ¼ tsp kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: red pepper flakes or lemon juice

Prep Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Scoop avocado into a bowl and mash with salt.
  3. Spread avocado evenly over toast.
  4. Season with pepper and optional toppings.
  5. Serve immediately.
